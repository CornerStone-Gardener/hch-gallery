## [STYLE SPEC]
Use warm cream, charcoal, and a restrained crimson with literary serif typography and fine ink-like rules.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Open sections with a drop cap or pull quote, preserve book-like margins, and use horizontal rules to separate evidence and commentary.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No parchment textures, quill imagery, ornate borders, dense manuscript pages, or crimson used as a background fill.
