## [STYLE SPEC]
Use a soft blue field, warm-white card, disciplined pastel pills, and friendly geometric sans type.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Anchor content inside one rounded frame; use five consistent edge pills as rhythm and keep diagrams simple and labeled.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No candy-color overload, nested cards, childish icons, weak contrast, or arbitrary pill dimensions.
