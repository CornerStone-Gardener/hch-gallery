## [STYLE SPEC]
Use a polished magazine composition with matte pink, charcoal editorial type, a refined serif/sans mix, and restrained handwritten notes.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Anchor a cutout subject centrally, distribute numbered side notes asymmetrically, and use crop-mark lines and one vertical edge caption.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No childish brights, flashy gradients, heavy shadows, excessive speech bubbles, or weak subject isolation.
