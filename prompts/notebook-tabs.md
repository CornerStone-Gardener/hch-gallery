## [STYLE SPEC]
Set a cream paper page on charcoal with elegant editorial headings and muted pastel index tabs.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Use tabs as section navigation, a consistent paper frame, subtle binder details, and generous lesson-content margins.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No photoreal stationery, excessive shadows, tiny vertical labels, random tab colors, or overcrowded notes.
