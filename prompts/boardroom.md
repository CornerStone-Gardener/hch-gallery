## [STYLE SPEC]
Use a white, navy, and steel-blue consulting system with neutral sans typography and hairline rules.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Lead every content slide with a full-sentence conclusion; use strict grids, sourced footers, restrained charts, and a small progress tracker.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No decorative imagery, gradients, shadows, rounded cards, vague titles, or more than two accents.
