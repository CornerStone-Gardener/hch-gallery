## [STYLE SPEC]
Use deep navy with cyan as the functional signal and magenta as a rare secondary event; keep typography crisp and futuristic.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Build on a faint technical grid, use particles only for depth, and connect claims through clean routes, nodes, and status markers.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No illegible glow, Matrix rain, gratuitous particles, neon on every element, or fake terminal content.
