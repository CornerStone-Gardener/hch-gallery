## [STYLE SPEC]
Use ecru or indigo grounds with ink-black or bengara-red type, flat woodblock-print color, and bold cropped compositions.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Let one large motif (wave, mountain, fabric pattern) anchor the composition; keep type minimal with a clear title/body split.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No photographic realism, cluttered multiple motifs, low-contrast red-on-red text, or generic clip-art waves.
