## [STYLE SPEC]
Compose a serious newspaper-inspired system with serif headlines, compact sans annotations, fine rules, and warm newsprint.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Use column logic, strong headline hierarchy, pull quotes, captioned charts, and explicit sources; preserve a clear reading order.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No faux-aged stains, novelty newspaper mastheads, dense unreadable columns, clip art, or unsourced figures.
