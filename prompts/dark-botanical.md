## [STYLE SPEC]
Use near-black, warm ivory, muted gold and pink, elegant serif display type, and blurred abstract color fields.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Center sparse content, balance soft corner shapes with thin vertical rules, and reserve italics for a signature phrase.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No literal botanical illustrations, bright saturation, heavy shadows, dense copy, or generic luxury gold gradients.
