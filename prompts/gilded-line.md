## [STYLE SPEC]
Use rich black or deep emerald with gold and silver accents, art-deco display type, and radiating symmetric geometric frames.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Center content inside a radial or fan-shaped ornamental frame; let one decorative motif bookend each slide, never more.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No generic gold gradients, cluttered ornamentation, low-contrast gold-on-gold text, or asymmetric deco motifs.
