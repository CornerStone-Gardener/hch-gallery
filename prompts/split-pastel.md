## [STYLE SPEC]
Divide the canvas between peach and lavender, using dark type and a few mint or yellow badges.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Keep a stable vertical split, use the right-side grid only as subtle structure, and place one clear action or takeaway per slide.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No pastel rainbow, noisy patterns, pill swarms, low-contrast copy, or symmetrical default composition.
