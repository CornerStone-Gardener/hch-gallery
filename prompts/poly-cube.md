## [STYLE SPEC]
Use faceted low-poly 3D geometry — rotating-cube logic in primary red, green, and blue — on white with bold black type.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Treat the cube or polygon cluster as a single 3x3 structural grid anchor; keep supporting text minimal and high-contrast.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No pastel recoloring, more than three primary accents at once, busy backgrounds, or crowded polygon fields.
