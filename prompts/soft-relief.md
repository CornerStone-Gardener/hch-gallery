## [STYLE SPEC]
Use an off-white canvas with soft embossed UI elements — light-highlight and shadow pairs that read as gently raised or pressed.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Treat every content block as a rounded relief card; keep contrast low but text legible, and never let more than one element look "pressed in" at once.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No harsh drop shadows, saturated colors, sharp corners, or more than two relief depths per slide.
