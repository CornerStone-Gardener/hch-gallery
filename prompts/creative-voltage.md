## [STYLE SPEC]
Combine electric blue, deep navy, neon yellow, geometric display type, and sparse halftone texture.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Use opposing split panels, mono metadata, a single neon badge, and controlled overlap to create forward motion.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No rainbow neon, illegible scripts, full-slide halftones, sticker clutter, or generic cyberpunk imagery.
