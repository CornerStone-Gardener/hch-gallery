## [STYLE SPEC]
Use warm cream, black, muted beige, expressive serif headlines, and clean sans body copy.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Center the principal editorial claim, counterbalance it with one geometric line-circle motif, and frame calls to action with simple borders.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No literal vintage illustrations, sepia filters, ornate frames, shadow-heavy cards, or generic motivational copy.
