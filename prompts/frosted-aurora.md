## [STYLE SPEC]
Use an aurora-gradient background seen through frosted glass, with white knockout or translucent type and soft bordered highlights.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Let the gradient carry the mood while type stays restrained and legible; one translucent panel per slide holds the message.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No harsh rainbow banding, illegible type over gradient, retro clip art, or more than one frosted panel.
