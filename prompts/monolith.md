## [STYLE SPEC]
Create expensive restraint: near-black fields, warm-white type, sparse silver rules, and rare serif moments.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Allow one idea, number, or image to dominate; use large margins, deliberate pauses, and minimal evidence blocks.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No neon, busy charts, decorative gradients, crowded logos, cheap gold effects, or multiple focal points.
