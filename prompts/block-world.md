## [STYLE SPEC]
Use isometric low-poly clay-rendered blocks on a soft beige or sky-blue field, with rounded bold type and matte lighting.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Build one isometric scene per slide as the focal explainer; keep labels short and let building-block shapes carry structure.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No photoreal 3D rendering, cluttered multi-scene slides, glossy plastic highlights, or tiny illegible labels.
