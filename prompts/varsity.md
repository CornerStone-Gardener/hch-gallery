## [STYLE SPEC]
Use collegiate block type, warm athletic colors, stitched-line accents, and bold score-card numerals.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Build diagonal momentum with large numbers, team-color bands, roster grids, and one rallying phrase per slide.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No trademarked team marks, mascot clip art, distressed texture overload, weak contrast, or cramped copy.
