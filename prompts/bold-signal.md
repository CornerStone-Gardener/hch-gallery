## [STYLE SPEC]
Use a charcoal gradient field, one orange signal card, white type, and heavy display typography.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Place section number top-left, navigation top-right, and the primary claim bottom-left on a precise grid.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No extra accent colors, card mosaics, decorative icons, soft hierarchy, or multiple hero messages.
