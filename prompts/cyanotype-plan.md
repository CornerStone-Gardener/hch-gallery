## [STYLE SPEC]
Use medium-blue blueprint fields with white line-drawing type and light-blue dimension annotations, drafting-font headlines.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Draw content like architectural or mechanical drawings — dimension lines, section marks, and uppercase labels on a visible grid.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No photographic textures, color outside the blue/white/light-blue system, decorative icons, or illegible dimension text.
