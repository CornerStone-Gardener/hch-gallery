## [STYLE SPEC]
Use a dark technical canvas with copper traces, signal-green states, monospaced labels, and schematic precision.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Route information along a visible grid; show architecture as labeled nodes and paths, and isolate one active route or component per slide.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No fake code walls, cyberpunk clutter, illegible wiring, 3D circuitry, or unexplained decoration.
