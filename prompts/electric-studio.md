## [STYLE SPEC]
Use a sharp white-and-electric-blue split with black anchors and confident Manrope typography.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Divide the canvas into two vertical panels, add one edge accent bar, and let a quote or claim span the hierarchy.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No gradients, excessive logos, rounded card grids, more than one accent, or equal-weight text blocks.
