## [STYLE SPEC]
Use an academic light theme with readable type, restrained blue emphasis, and high-clarity diagrams.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Present one teaching point per slide; pair concise claims with a single exhibit, label every axis, and reserve a consistent citation footer.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No bullet walls, tiny citations, decorative stock photos, unexplained equations, or low-contrast text.
