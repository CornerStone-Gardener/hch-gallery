## [STYLE SPEC]
Use cyber-yellow and black with cyan accents, stencil or angular sans headlines, and hazard-label design language.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Use warning-stripe bands, barcode-style metadata, and one glitch accent — never let stripes overwhelm the message.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No literal barcodes without meaning, excessive glitch noise, soft rounded type, or pastel softening.
