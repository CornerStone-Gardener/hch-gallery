## [STYLE SPEC]
Use cream paper with ink-blue text, flat gold and red risograph-style misregistration, and rough newsprint texture.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Let bold sans or handwritten headlines bleed slightly off-register; keep ink-bleed texture as atmosphere, not decoration.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No digital-clean gradients, glossy finishes, more than three riso colors per slide, or perfectly aligned registration.
