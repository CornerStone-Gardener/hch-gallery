## [STYLE SPEC]
Use near-black glass panels with heavy backdrop blur and white text floating on frosted, semi-transparent surfaces.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Keep hierarchy legible through one blur depth per panel, generous negative space, and disciplined alignment; avoid stacking more than two glass layers.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No rainbow refraction, illegible low-contrast text on glass, cluttered thumbnail grids, or decorative icons.
