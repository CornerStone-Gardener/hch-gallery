## [STYLE SPEC]
Use raw system-default UI energy: giant monospace type, unstyled rectangles, and pasted-in imagery with visible CSS-frame lines.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Keep a strict title/body split, let monospace numerals carry data, and use pure blue or red only as a single warning-level accent.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No rounded corners, gradients, decorative icons, soft shadows, or more than one accent color.
