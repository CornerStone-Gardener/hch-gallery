## [STYLE SPEC]
Use pure white, black, and one signal red with a visible Bauhaus-informed grid and strong sans hierarchy.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Align asymmetrically, expose the grid through rules and geometry, and make one typographic or data statement dominant.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No centered hero defaults, rounded UI components, gradients, decorative photos, or more than one accent color.
