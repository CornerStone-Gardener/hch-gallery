## [STYLE SPEC]
Use high-fashion magazine layout logic in pure black and white — extreme cropping, generous whitespace, and one dominant Didot-style serif moment.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Let scale contrast do the work: one oversized headline, one small caption block, and nothing competing for attention.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No color accents, busy patterns, small evenly-sized text blocks, or centered symmetrical defaults.
