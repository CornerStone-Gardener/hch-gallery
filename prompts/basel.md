## [STYLE SPEC]
Treat oversized black typography and one red accent as the primary visual language on white.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Use asymmetric Swiss grids, hard alignment, extreme scale contrast, and at most one supporting image or diagram per slide.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No centered templates, soft gradients, decorative illustrations, rounded UI cards, or timid type scale.
