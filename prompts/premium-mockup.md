## [STYLE SPEC]
Use a clean studio system with white, pale gray, and black stages plus restrained electric-purple and acid-yellow actions.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Make the product screen occupy 70–80% of the canvas; use soft grounded shadows, precise UI spacing, cropped scale, and short spec labels.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No unlicensed brand-device replicas, pixelated screens, excessive gloss, floating UI clutter, or more than two accents.
