## [STYLE SPEC]
Mix smartphone-first business editorial energy with Swiss asymmetry, jet-black type, and electric yellow or alert red emphasis.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Use one message per slide, 10:1 headline-to-body scale, monochrome cutouts, sharp grids, and deliberate alternation between dense type and empty space.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No markdown symbols in displayed copy, centered default layouts, multiple competing accents, or long descriptive titles.
