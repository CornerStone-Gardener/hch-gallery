## [STYLE SPEC]
Use soft pastel clay-like 3D UI — capsules, puffed rectangles, spheres, and floating cards with gentle lighting.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Center content with generous padding so shapes feel like they're floating; keep one clay element as the focal object per slide.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No sharp edges, saturated neon pastels, dense text on clay shapes, or more than three floating objects per slide.
