## [STYLE SPEC]
Build a dark SaaS pitch with disciplined cyan-to-violet light accents, crisp sans type, and luminous data emphasis.
## [LAYOUT RULES]
Keep one claim per slide; alternate hero statements, product frames, metric bands, and simple proof diagrams with generous margins.
## [FORBIDDEN]
No rainbow gradients, glassmorphism stacks, tiny dashboard text, generic icons, or ornamental glow.
