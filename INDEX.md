## Family coverage

Each required family has three primary selections, matched against each card's actual `tags:` field: consulting/exec (`boardroom`, `electric-studio`, `swiss-modern`), pitch (`aurora`, `monolith`, `bold-signal`), tech/product (`circuit`, `premium-mockup`, `terminal-green`), editorial (`broadsheet`, `modern-newspaper`, `paper-and-ink`), creative/portfolio (`basel`, `mature-magazine`, `creative-voltage`), education (`seminar`, `notebook-tabs`, `pastel-geometry`), high-energy/pop (`varsity`, `athletic-energy`, `neon-cyber`), and minimal-premium (`swiss-modern`, `monolith`, `dark-botanical`). Cross-family tags intentionally overlap; `split-pastel` and `vintage-editorial` are secondary options within pitch/high-energy-pop and editorial/creative-portfolio respectively.
